Frequently Asked Questions

The Indian mobile market is booming, and users get so many options to choose from that they get confused. Some of the best smartphone brands in the country include Apple, Samsung, OnePlus, Xiaomi, vivo, and OPPO. Moreover, handsets from any of these brands come with guaranteed performance and reliability.

Most mobile applications and software are resource hungry. Therefore, we advise you to get a phone with at least 2 GB RAM, which is sufficient for regular usage. However, if you are an avid gamer or frequently stream HD content, we recommend a handset with at least 3 GB to 4 GB RAM to ensure a smooth, lag-free performance.

Samsung A12 and vivo Y21 are two of the best handsets for students in India. Both these handsets pack the Mediatek Helio 35 processor, powerful 5,000 mAh capacity batteries, and large displays. While Samsung A12 has a quad rear camera setup, vivo Y21 has a dual rear camera with a 13MP primary sensor.

OnePlus 9 Pro 5G and Reno7 Pro 5G are two of the best gaming phones in India. Both phones have 12 GB RAM to handle the latest AAA gaming titles. In addition, these phones pack powerful 4,500 mAh capacity batteries to ensure long hours of gaming on a single charge.
